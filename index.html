About Me

My first experience with technology was in an elementary computer class where I explored the applications and played typing games on the iMac G3. Using the computer came easy to me, but my peers often had complications. I discovered I had a skill for troubleshooting after resolving their issues and I soon became the go-to person whenever my classmates, friends, family needed help. I enjoyed helping others, so I decided to participate in community efforts such as teaching kids to read, volunteering to raise funds for cancer research, cleaning and clearing up my high school campus.

Seeing the results after these acts gave me a feeling of accomplishment, and it still serves as my primary motivation today. Creating a safe and comfortable environment is important to me as well, therefore when I am in a team I try to build relationships with them to empathize with their perspectives and understand their circumstances. I believe having this mindset leads to more growth which is my main goal in life.
